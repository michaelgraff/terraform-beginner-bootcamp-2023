Easy Star All Stars

Easy Star All-Stars is a reggae collective founded in 1997 by Michael Goldwasser, Eric Smith, Lem Oppenheimer and Remy Gerstein of New York City-based Easy Star Records.

The band is best known for its reinterpretations of classic albums in dub reggae style.

If you are a fan of reggae you will not want to miss this band. The way they choose to reinterpret some classic recordings is both extremely creative and highly entertaining.

Discography

The band has released a total of eight albums so far, with two of them being original content and six of them being complete remakes of classic albums from start to finish

Their first album entitled "Dub Side of the Moon" was released in 2003, and is a complete cover of Pink Floyd's "Dark Side of the Moon"

Their second album, and perhaps my favorite from this band is "Radiodread, released in 2006, which is a remake of the classic Radiohead album "OK Computer"

"Easy Star's Lonely Hearts Dub Band" released in 2009 is a tour de force tribute to the seminal "Sgt. Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band" by the Beatles.

The band's most recent release, from 2023 is "Ziggy Stardub", a remake of the classic David Bowie album "Ziggy Stardust".
